How do automated accessibility checkers compare?

We ran an audit to see what issues the different accessibility testing tools pick up. We deliberately introduced {{ analysis.totals.total }} accessibility barriers to a page of content, and ran them through {{ analysis.counts | length }} accessibility checkers. We also had a look at features like usability and cost.

You can use our audit to help choose the best accessibility testing tool for your service.

Read our blog post on what we found.

Project available on Github. We welcome contributions.
