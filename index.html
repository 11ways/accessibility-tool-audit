Accessibility tool audit
  1. Overview

How do automated accessibility checkers compare?

We ran an audit to see what issues the different accessibility testing tools pick up. We deliberately introduced 143 accessibility barriers to a page of content, and ran them through 10 accessibility checkers.

We also had a look at features like usability and cost.

You can use our audit to help choose the best accessibility testing tool for your service.

Read our blog post on what we found.

Project available on Github. We welcome contributions.

What we found

In our tests, the best performing tool found 37% of the barriers we introduced. Whereas the worst performing tool only picked up 9% of the barriers.
